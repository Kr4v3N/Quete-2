About Us

Since 2003:A driving force in the circus art reinvention movement, Wild Circus has been creating award-winning entertainment content for nearly 14 years and ranks among the world’s leading contemporary circuses.Wild Circus has taken part in numerous prestigious international festivals and has seduced both New York’s Broadway and London’s West End. Its productions are crafted for a wide range of audiences and have been embraced by over fifty cultures. In the last 14 years,Wild Circus’s twelve productions have proudly boasted more than 1,500 performances in over 140 cities and have been seen by over 1,3 million spectators.Ninth and eleventh touring shows under the Wild Circus banner. In addition to its touring shows, more than 747 designed events have taken place worldwide.

International Headquarters:Wild Circus International Headquarters, located in Lyon, is a laboratory of creativity, where the world’s best creative minds, craftspeople, experts in various domains, and artists can collaborate on creative projects. By assuming the roles of catalyst and unifier,Wild Circus is able to reinvent itself with each new chapter of its history.
